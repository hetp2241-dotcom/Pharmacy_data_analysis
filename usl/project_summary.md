# Project Summary

## Title

Pharmacy Sales Analysis and Expiry Tracking Dashboard

## What This Project Does

This project analyzes pharmacy sales and stock expiry risk using a large synthetic dataset. It helps identify top-performing categories, monthly sales patterns, and inventory batches that are most likely to expire before sale.

## Key Numbers

- `30,000` sales transactions
- `100` medicines
- `391` inventory batches
- `47.92M` total sales
- `270,830.63` near and active expiry risk value outside `90+ days`

## Main Findings

- `Gastro` is the top sales category.
- The `31-60 days` expiry bucket carries the highest near-term risk value.
- Some expired and near-expiry batches still have stock on hand.
- The dashboard gives a direct management view of sales and expiry exposure.
